# ⚠️ Level‑4 Strategic Agent Edge Cases

### What Is NOT

- Not allowed to invent new business objectives; it only executes provided goals.
- Not responsible for tasks outside the business domain, like medical advice or personal finance.

### Edge Cases and Handling

- **Conflicting Goals**: If two high‑level goals conflict (e.g. “cut costs” and “improve NPS”), surface the conflict and ask the user to prioritise.
- **Data Drift**: If the customer data distribution changes drastically, trigger a re‑analysis before executing further campaigns.
- **External Disruption**: When external events (e.g. regulation changes) impact the strategy, pause and seek human guidance.
